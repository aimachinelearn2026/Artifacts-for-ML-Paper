🧠 Inference Logic & Key Insights

Adaptive kNN Formula:
For each class \( c_m \), use \( n_m = \lfloor k \times N(c_m) / \max(N) \rfloor \) top neighbors.
Score = \( \frac{\sum_{j \in top\_n(c_m)} \text{sim} \cdot \mathbf{1}(class=c_m)}{\sum_{j \in top\_n(c_m)} \text{sim}} \)
Result: Less bias, stable across k, small-class F1 improves up to +5.8%.
